‚≠ê The Force and Identity

In the Star Wars universe, the discovery of Force sensitivity often marks a fundamental change in identity. Jedi traditionally leave their birth names behind, adopting new identities that reflect their spiritual journey. Sith similarly transform their names into titles of power and fear. This tradition of name-changing reflects the transformative nature of embracing the Force, whether for light or dark purposes.

Even non-Force users may adopt new names when joining significant organizations like the Rebel Alliance or Imperial military, showing how the galaxy's conflicts shape personal identity.
